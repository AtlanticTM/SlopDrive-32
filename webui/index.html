stroke · shape

Make one control drift over a repeating cycle of strokes — e.g. max depth ramps gradually to a minimum, holds, then climbs back. Each bar in the staircase is one stroke. Pick the control, drag the step handles to set how many strokes each phase lasts (to min / at min / to max / at max), and the left fader (amplitude) for how far it drifts — 100 means no change. The offset marker on the lower track shifts where the cycle starts, so two controls can drift out of step — e.g. depth dips while speed is still climbing.
